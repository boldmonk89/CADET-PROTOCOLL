# CADET PROTOCOL: THE ALPHA VISION ROADMAP

This document outlines the high-precision medical & psychological capabilities planned for future iterations.

## 1. Computer Vision (External Physicals)
The AI will guide the user through a series of physical poses to detect discrepancies in:
- **Carrying Angle**: Detection of Cubitus Valgus / Varus via elbow extension.
- **Flat Foot**: Arch analysis during static stance.
- **Knock Knees / Bow Legs**: Genu Valgum and Genu Varum detection via knee-to-ankle gap analysis.
- **Kyphosis / Scoliosis**: Spinal alignment check from lateral and posterior views.
- **Postural Control**: AI instructions ("Stand straight", "Fold hands") to verify muscle tone and skeletal alignment.

## 2. Sensory Intelligence
Advanced testing methodologies for sensory acuity:
### Vision (Color Perception)
- **CP-Matrix**: Automated Ishihara plate sequence.
- **Dynamics**: Plates flash for exactly 6 seconds before transitioning.
- **Voice Response**: User speaks the digit; AI captures voice-to-text for judgment.

### Hearing (Whisper Test)
- **Masking Technique**: Playing binaural audio (e.g., pencil scribbling in left ear, whisper in right).
- **Measurement**: User repeats the whispered phrase; AI correlates against audio sample accuracy.

## 3. Internal Integrity & Referral System
Since the platform cannot perform radiological or invasive checks, a specific "Internal Audit Section" will be established:
- **Scope**: Kidney, Testicular, and Cardiac issues.
- **Mechanism**: Questionnaire-based symptom tracking.
- **Routing**: Automated referrals to **Preferred Command Hospitals** for these specific physical clearances.

## 4. Interaction Model
- **Voice Instruction**: The AI system will actively speak to the user, providing step-by-step guidance.
- **Real-time Feedback**: "Move closer", "Turn 90 degrees", "Background too bright".
